## Full Description — ES (4000 chars max)

**KIRU+** es la plataforma de educación quirúrgica diseñada para médicos en formación: residentes de cirugía general, internos de pregrado y cirujanos generales certificados por el CMCG.

Construida sobre evidencia sólida: guías ATLS®, clasificación AAST, Guías de Tokio, lineamientos de la Asociación Mexicana de Cirugía General y el Nuevo Tratado de Cirugía 2025.

---

### K-Tools — Calculadoras clínicas offline

Toma decisiones con evidencia, sin necesitar internet:

- **Alvarado / AIR** — probabilidad diagnóstica de apendicitis aguda
- **ASA** — clasificación de riesgo anestésico preoperatorio
- **Child-Pugh** — función hepática y pronóstico en cirrosis (Clase A / B / C)
- **BISAP** — estratificación de gravedad en pancreatitis aguda (0–5)
- **SOFA** — puntuación de fallo multiorgánico (6 sistemas fisiológicos)
- **APACHE II** — predicción de mortalidad en Unidad de Cuidados Intensivos
- **Marshall** — fallo orgánico específico en pancreatitis aguda
- **RIPASA** — score diagnóstico de apendicitis (validado en población latinoamericana)
- **p-POSSUM** — predicción de morbilidad y mortalidad quirúrgica (regresión logística Portsmouth)

Todas funcionan completamente offline. Sin publicidad. Sin datos que se vendan.

---

- Protocolos ATLS y manejo del trauma

- Preparación para exámenes de especialidad

Respuestas en tiempo real con streaming. Contexto conversacional persistente. Entrenado para el lenguaje y contexto de la cirugía latinoamericana.

## Full Description — EN (4000 chars max)

**KIRU+** is a surgical education platform built for medical professionals in training: general surgery residents, pre-degree interns, and board-certified surgeons.

Grounded in solid evidence: ATLS® guidelines, AAST classification, Tokyo Guidelines, and the most current surgical references for the Latin American context.

---

### K-Tools — Offline Clinical Calculators

Make evidence-based decisions anywhere, without internet access:

- **Alvarado / AIR** — diagnostic probability for acute appendicitis
- **ASA** — preoperative anesthetic risk classification
- **Child-Pugh** — hepatic function and prognosis in cirrhosis (Class A / B / C)
- **BISAP** — severity stratification in acute pancreatitis (0–5)
- **SOFA** — multi-organ failure score (6 physiological systems)
- **APACHE II** — ICU mortality prediction
- **Marshall** — organ-specific failure score in acute pancreatitis
- **RIPASA** — appendicitis diagnostic score (validated in Latin American populations)
- **p-POSSUM** — surgical morbidity and mortality prediction (Portsmouth logistic regression)

All calculators work 100% offline. No ads. No data selling.

---

### Dr. Kapibaya — AI Surgical Tutor

Your virtual senior resident, available around the clock. Ask about:

- ATLS protocols and trauma management
- Diagnostic decision-making in the acute abdomen
- Clinical scale interpretation and underlying evidence
- Surgical pharmacology and perioperative antibiotics
- Specialty board examination preparation

Real-time streaming responses. Persistent conversational memory. Trained in the language and context of Latin American surgical practice.

---

### K-CORTEX — AI-Assisted Clinical Analysis

Enter clinical case findings and receive a structured analysis: differential diagnosis, suggested severity scoring, management plan, and bibliographic references. Ideal for surgical case conferences and academic rounds.

---

### Surgical Logbook

Document each procedure with precision:

- Date, procedure type, and complexity level
- Resident role (assistant / primary surgeon)
- Intraoperative findings and complications
- Personal notes and postoperative follow-up

All records securely synchronized to the cloud and accessible from any device.

---

### KIRU Academy

- Structured lessons by subspecialty: trauma, hepatobiliary, colorectal, abdominal wall
- Multiple-choice quizzes with immediate feedback and evidence-based explanations
- Clinical pearls curated by surgeons with teaching experience
